Toyota Production and Sales

Production Sales Country Map

Map above compares sales and production of Toyota cars for each country. Throughout the year Japan has been the main producer averaging 3.1 million cars per year and peak of 3.4 million cars produced in 2012. Followed by China increasing production every year, The United States, Canada, Thailand and Indonesia.

Looking at sales, Toyota main consumer throughout the 12 year period is The United States averaging 1.1 million sales and peak of 2.4 million sales on 2016. Followed by annual demand increase in China, Southeast asia, Brazil and South America.

Grayed area means that the deviation of sales and production value on that country is very small, Select Category to production/ sales to see the actual value of each category.
